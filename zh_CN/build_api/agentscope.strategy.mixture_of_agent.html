Your task is to synthesize these responses into a single, high-quality response. Your response should not simply replicate the given answers but should offer a refined, accurate, and comprehensive reply to the instruction. Ensure your response is well-structured, coherent, and adheres to the highest standards of accuracy and reliability.\n\nResponses from models:', show_internal: bool = False)[源代码]
